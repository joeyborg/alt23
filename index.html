ALT23

Title

Emojis are the only things left for expression

Bio

We are a newly formed artist collective interested in the techno-human relations with respect to contemporary image-making. This is our first submission, and we are presenting our first portfolio of works.

Statement

Our interest is to venture into new territory of contemporary image-making by co-authoring works with technology. We draw inspiration from content that is changing and affecting the way we live. The presented images from part of our first series ‘Emojis are the only things left for expression’ and were made by drawing using a digital canvas that was programmed to only contain emoticons as a palette.
